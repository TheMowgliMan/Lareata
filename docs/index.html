BEYOND
THE BLOCK.

Lareata is a sandbox-style game inspired by the likes of Terraria and Minecraft. However, we aren't imitators. Lareata will be a game completely different from those two, including things such as:

  • Many Meaningful Biomes
  • Dozens of Enemies to Defeat
  • Many Powerful Bosses
  • Powerful Weapons and Armor
  • And more!

You can also do things like:

  • Build Architectural Masterpieces
  • Mine Thousands of Tiles
  • Destroy the World
  • Remove all Evil
  • And more!
