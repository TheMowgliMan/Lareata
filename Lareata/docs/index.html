WELCOME TO
A NEW WORLD

Lareata is a sandbox-style game inspired by the likes of Terraria and Minecraft. However, instead of being confused because you didn't get any crafting advice, you'll find yourself being wrapped up in an epic adventure as you try and find out who you are, where you are, and what happened to you. Most importantly, you will be faced with the daunting task of finding your place in an inherently evil and corrupted world. Both light and darkness will be three strides behind you, chasing you, so you don't want to stumble and fall...

It also has these features and possibilities:

  • Build Architectural Masterpieces
  • Mine Thousands of Tiles
  • Destroy the World
  • Remove all Evil
  • Many Meaningful Biomes
  • Dozens of Enemies to Defeat
  • Many Powerful Bosses
  • Powerful Weapons and Armor
  • And more!
